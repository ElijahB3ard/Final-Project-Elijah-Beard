## Joja Influence:
Your joja influence/ trade off system is realted to both your friendship points and your total score. The higher your joja influence, the more your friendship points decrease, and the less points you get. Depending on your influence by the end, you can get different endings

## Win/ Lose:
The basic win lose condition is just whether you finish the bundle in time or not. If you don't finish it by the end of x days, you lose, and you get the option to finish the game when you finish the bundle. After you finish the bundle, you can still walk around and get some custom lines from NPCs congradulating you, however, if you run out of time doing that, you still win the game.
